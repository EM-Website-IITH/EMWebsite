Vision

To become a department of international repute in the area of entrepreneurial education, training and research.


Aim, Focus and Scope of the Department

To nurture entrepreneurial motivation and skills among young graduates and to produce high quality research in the areas of entrepreneurship and management.




The Director’s Message

Indian Institute of Technology Hyderabad, with its fast-paced growth in quality research and teaching has emerged as one of the top 10 engineering institutes in India in the QS-2021 World Rankings. By prioritizing highly futuristic and novel areas like climate change and AI, IITH has the early movers’ advantage of becoming a trendsetter in various other areas as well. We are also in an advantageous position of becoming a pedestal of interdisciplinary research and teaching by synergizing the strengths of our faculty in science, engineering, liberal arts and design. With the prime focus on entrepreneurship and management, I strongly believe that this department has great potential to nurture young entrepreneurs who can contribute to the economic and social development of the country.
~ Prof. B. S. Murty



The Head’s Message

The Department of Entrepreneurship and Management was established in 2021 as part of IIT Hyderabad that takes pride in promoting innovation and interdisciplinarity. The department has tremendous potential to become a pioneer in entrepreneurship education and research. The academic programmes pursued by the department are predominantly focussed on nurturing entrepreneurship and moving from the paradigm of job seekers to that of job creators. The department also takes keen interest in 'Management' contextualized for 'New Age Organizations' in both research and teaching. The department welcomes students, researchers and industry experts to partner with us in different roles in our pursuit.
~ Dr. Nakul Parameswar

Minor in Entrepreneurship


B.Tech students who are in their 5th Semester are eligible to register for the Minor in Entrepreneurship. To fulfill the minor students have to undergo 12 credits of course work. Courses will cover concepts related to entrepreneurship and Management.

Online Symposium on 'Deeptech Entrepreneurship: Opportunities and Gaps'


Department of Entrepreneurship and Management, Indian Institute of Technology Hyderabad is organizing a half-day online symposium on "Deeptech Entrepreneurship: Opportunities and Gaps" on 16th March 2022. The link to register for the online symposium is https://bit.ly/sde-iith

The symposium includes a keynote address by Mr Sanjeev Narsipur, Managing Director Technology Consulting-Platform Advisory, Industry & Function Platforms Group & Cloud Advisor at Accenture along with Panel Discussions on "Role of Deeptech Entrepreneurship in Turbulent Times" and "Deeptech Entrepreneurship and Sustainability".

Executive Development Program


IITH, Dept. of Entrepreneurship in collaboration with Business Design Labs offered a unique action learning program on Business Model Innovation for Business Leaders, Entrepreneurs and Intrapreneurs.

The 30 hour certificate course helped participants identify opportunities to innovate their business model and conduct business experiments using Design Thinking Methods.

Certificate course on Deeptech Entrepreneurship


iTIC and Department of Entrepreneurship and Management are together running a 5-day certificate course on the theme of "Deeptech Entrepreneurship" from 21st to 25th February 2022.

This course is aimed at aspiring entrepreneurs and intended to give them exposure to basic concepts of Business Models, financial modelling, branding, sales and pricing strategies, IP etc. It will also touch upon the upcoming technology trends in AI/ML, Blockchain, Industry 4.0, Manufacturing, Mobility, Healthtech etc.

Workshop on Project Management for Entrepreneurship


Department of Entrepreneurship and Management is organizing an offline workshop on project management for the students undergoing MTech. in Techno-Entrepreneurship on 24 September 2022 to be conducted at the IIT Hyderabad campus. The workshop is going to be facilitated by Dr Smiju Sudevan, Assistant Professor in Information Systems at Cochin University of Science and Technology.
